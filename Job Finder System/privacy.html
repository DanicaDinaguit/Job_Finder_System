Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our job finder system.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Your name, email address, and contact information.
  • Your educational background and work experience.
  • Any additional information you provide in your job seeker profile.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To match you with relevant job opportunities based on your skills and preferences.
  • To communicate with you regarding job recommendations, updates, or notifications.
  • To improve our job finder system and provide a personalized user experience.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with our trusted partners and service providers who assist us in delivering our services.
